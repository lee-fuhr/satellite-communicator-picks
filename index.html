How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or timed trials. Instead, our picks are based on months of reading user forums, long-term owner feedback on Reddit and Backpacking Light, and published reviews from trusted sources like Outdoor Gear Lab and The Trek. We also consult with satellite communication veterans who have used these devices in real emergencies. What matters most to us is reliability when it counts – network coverage, message delivery rates, battery longevity, and how well the companion app works in the field. We systematically track reported failures: SOS buttons that don’t register, subscription activation headaches, and hardware that breaks after a few trips. The devices we recommend have a proven track record over multiple seasons. We also consider the total cost of ownership beyond the device price – because a cheap unit with an expensive mandatory plan is no bargain.
