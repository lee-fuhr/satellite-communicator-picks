Our verdict

Choosing the right satellite communicator comes down to how you adventure. For most people, the Garmin inReach Messenger offers the best balance of messaging capability, battery life, and usability at a reasonable price. If global coverage or simplicity matters more, the ZOLEO is a worthy alternative on a budget, the Motorola Defy Satellite Link is a surprisingly capable entry point, and the inReach Mini 2 delivers a featherweight option. And for those who want navigation built in, the inReach Explorer+ is the ultimate companion. No matter which you pick, having a satellite communicator along is one of the smartest safety investments you can make.
