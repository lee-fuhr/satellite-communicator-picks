Satellite communicators have become essential gear for anyone venturing beyond cell service. Whether you are hiking a remote trail, backcountry skiing, or sailing offshore, a good device lets you stay in touch, share your location, and trigger an SOS if things go wrong. Some units offer two-way texting and smartphone pairing, while others focus on simple SOS and preset messages. You will also want to consider subscription costs, because the monthly plan can quickly exceed the price of the hardware.
